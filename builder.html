B U I L D E R

Build is an application designed to assist people stop procrastinating and start getting things done. Builder does this by encouraging collaboration with friends, family and workmates.

With Builder, you will be asked to create and manage goals. As you complete the tasks you set for yourself, you will be building an amazing world.
